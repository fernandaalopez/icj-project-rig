Chloé

Designed by Stella McCartney

Chloé’s Spring/Summer 2001 collection captured a sense of effortless sophistication, blending romantic femininity with a youthful, carefree spirit. Flowing fabrics, soft palettes, and playful, breezy designs made this collection a timeless representation of chic minimalism. The subtle flirtation with retro influences gave it an enduring appeal, bridging past and future with an understated absurdity that felt delightfully modern.
